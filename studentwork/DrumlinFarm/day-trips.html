Farm and Nature Programs

Drumlin Farm's nature and agriculture programs are the perfect way to explore the farm, forest, fields, and wetlands around us. Join our teacher-naturalists as they guide your experience with hands-on activities, hikes, farm chores, and wildlife discoveries. With hundreds of Drumlin program options for children, families, adults, school groups as well as clubs, the possibilities are endless!

Bird Watching

Download a bird checklist before your visit to Drumlin Farm and bring it with you for a convenient way to keep track of birds you see.

After your visit, you can use your checklist to contribute to our knowledge of birdlife at our wildlife sanctuaries. Submit your observations at www.massaudubon.org/ebird.

This is one of twelve Mass Audubon Quests, designed to help you discover the special stories of our wildlife sanctuaries. Navigate the rhyming clues and learn about Drumlin Farm’s wildlife, habitats, and history. Follow the clues and you will end up at one of the most beautiful spaces in the Drumlin Farm sanctuary, hidden from the farmyard but within an easy, one hour family walk.

You can download this Quest here, or pick
up a printed copy at the sanctuary. Enjoy your Drumlin Farm adventure!
