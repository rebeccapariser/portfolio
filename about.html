Hello!

I am a UI / UX designer with expertise in product and experience design. I love blending analytical and creative thinking to tackle complex challenges and design beautiful solutions that make a difference in people’s lives.

I am currently in the process of updating my website. Please forgive the unpolished experience, and check back soon for new content. Thank you for your patience!

Background

For the last three years I’ve worked at Big Tomorrow, designing digital products and experiences in the healthcare, education and entertainment industries. I’ve had experience working alone and collaborating with teams of up to 6 designers, designing for everyone from 4-year-olds learning to read to elderly hip- and knee-surgery patients, and designing a product from beginning stages of brainstorming and user research through to the end, with delivery of complete web and mobile comps and QA with developers.

In addition, I have worked for Cloud City Development as a UI / UX designer, interned at Amazon Lab126 on the Fire TV design team, and worked for a variety of freelance clients on logo, web and presentation design projects.

I received my MFA in Graphic Design from the Academy of Art University in 2014, and my BA in 2010 from Stanford University, in Comparative Literature and Creative Writing. I am a member of AIGA SF, and have taken classes including Tufte’s Presenting Data and Information, and Type@Cooper Union’s 10-week course on Principles of Typeface Design.
